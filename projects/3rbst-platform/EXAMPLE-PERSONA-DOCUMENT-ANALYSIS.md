## 🎯 **Key Differences in AI Analysis by Persona**

### **Content Depth**
- **Trial Users**: Basic, essential info only
- **Power Users**: Comprehensive, technical analysis
- **Specialists**: Expert-level, niche-focused
- **At-Risk**: Simplified with re-engagement hooks
- **Occasional**: Balanced, practical focus

### **Language Complexity**
- **Trial Users**: Simple Arabic, minimal German terms
- **Power Users**: Mixed technical terminology
- **Specialists**: Heavy use of German legal/tax terms
- **At-Risk**: Friendly, encouraging tone
- **Occasional**: Clear, straightforward language

### **Upselling Approach**
- **Trial Users**: Direct "buy credits" prompt
- **Power Users**: Status confirmation, no upsell
- **Specialists**: Category-specific features
- **At-Risk**: Special offers, discounts
- **Occasional**: Soft suggestion of packages

### **Additional Features**
- **Trial Users**: Welcome messages, tutorials
- **Power Users**: Statistics, comparisons, optimization tips
- **Specialists**: Legal references, success metrics
- **At-Risk**: What's new, special offers
- **Occasional**: Usage tracking, gentle nudges
