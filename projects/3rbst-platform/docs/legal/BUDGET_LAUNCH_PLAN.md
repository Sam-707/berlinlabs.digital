## 📄 **FREE TEMPLATE RESOURCES**

### **Impressum (Required by Law):**
**Free Generator:** impressum-generator.de
**Required fields:**
- Full name and business address
- Contact details (email, phone)
- Business registration number
- Tax number (when received)

### **Privacy Policy (GDPR Required):**
**Free Template:** datenschutz-generator.de
**Must include:**
- What data you collect (phone numbers, document images)
- Legal basis for processing (Art. 6 GDPR)
- How long you store data (24 hours)
- User rights (access, deletion, portability)
- Third parties involved (OpenAI, Supabase, PayPal)

### **Terms of Service:**
**Free Template:** agb-template.de
**Must include:**
- Service description and limitations
- Payment terms and refund policy
- User obligations
- Liability limitations
