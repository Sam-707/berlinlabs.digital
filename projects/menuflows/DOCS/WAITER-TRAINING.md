# Waiter Training — MenuFlows Handshake Workflow

## Purpose
Confirm customer orders, assign the correct table, and fire tickets to the kitchen.

---

## Quick SOP (Step-by-Step)

1. **Log in**
   - Screen: **Staff Authentication**
   - Enter your 4-digit PIN

2. **Open the Handshake Queue**
   - Screen: **Manager Portal**
   - Tap **Handshake Queue**
   - If you see “Incoming Order,” you have new codes to confirm

3. **Select a pending code**
   - Screen: **Pending Handshakes**
   - Tap the code that matches the customer’s device
   - You’ll see item count on the right

4. **Assign a table**
   - Screen: **Select Table**
   - Tap the correct table number (01–20)

5. **Confirm & Fire**
   - Screen: **Match Confirmed**
   - Review items quickly
   - Tap **Confirm & Fire**
   - Order status becomes **Confirmed** and is sent to kitchen

---

## What You’re Responsible For
- Verifying the **handshake code**
- Assigning the **correct table number**
- Sending the order to the kitchen with **Confirm & Fire**

## What You’re NOT Responsible For
- Changing cooking status
- Clearing tables
- Editing menu items

---

- “Incoming Order”

**Pending Handshakes**
- “Incoming Code”
- “Tap to Assign Table”

**Select Table**
- “Service Mode”
- “Select Table”

**Match Confirmed**
- “Match confirmed with customer device”
- “Confirm & Fire”

---

## If Something Looks Wrong
- **No pending codes?** Wait for a guest to generate one.
- **Wrong table selected?** Go back and re-select before Confirm & Fire.
- **Customer unsure?** Ask them to show their handshake code screen.

---

## Success Checklist
- Code matched
- Table assigned
- Order fired to kitchen
