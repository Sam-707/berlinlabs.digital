```
MenuFlows — a calm digital menu experience for restaurants.

```

---

## Why this works

- Matches calm tone of cover
- Gives one clear action
- Doesn't oversell
- Makes cover feel intentional, not decorative

---

## Sanity checklist (stop when all true)

- [ ] Cover contains **no buttons**
- [ ] Cover makes **no promises**
- [ ] Cover points to **Featured**, not "click here"
- [ ] Pinned post contains **one link, one artifact**
- [ ] Nothing mentions margins, AI-native, or services as pillars
- [ ] You feel *slightly bored* looking at it
  → this is good (means it's calm)

---

## Lock-in rule

**Do not iterate on this again until you have:**
- at least 5 MenuFlows conversations, OR
- one real restaurant using the demo

Design freezes are a superpower.
