# MenuFlows Business Model

> **Service**: Digital QR-based menu and ordering platform for restaurants
> **Target Market**: 50,000+ restaurants in Germany (DACH region)
> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-11

---

## Executive Summary

**MenuFlows** is a B2B SaaS platform that replaces paper menus with QR code-based digital ordering. Restaurants get a modern digital menu, customers order via QR scan, and orders go directly to the kitchen.

**Key Metrics**:
- **Market Size**: ~50,000 independent restaurants in Germany
- **Pain Point**: Expensive apps, paper menus outdated daily
- **Solution**: QR menus at €29-49/month (no app needed)
- **Gross Margin**: ~85%
- **Year 1 Revenue Potential**: €9,000-21,000 MRR

---

## Problem Statement

### The Pain Point

**For Restaurants**:
- Paper menus are expensive to print
- Menu changes require reprinting
- Can't update prices instantly
- No customer data/analytics
- Waiter dependency for ordering

**Current Solutions**:

| Solution | Pros | Cons |
|-----------|-------|------|
| Paper menus | Familiar | Outdated, wasteful, no data |
| Own app | Full control | €10K-50K development, low adoption |
| Delivery platforms | Exposure | High fees (15-30%), expensive for customers |
| Competing QR solutions | Cheaper | Still €29-79/mo, often complex |

**MenuFlows Advantage**:
- Affordable (€29-49/mo vs €79+ competitors)
- Self-service onboarding (instant)
- German + English (unique in market)
- Advanced modifier system (customizations)
- Multi-location support
