# 3rbst Business Model

> **Service**: WhatsApp-based German document translation for Arabic speakers
> **Target Market**: Arabic speakers in Germany (2M+ population)
> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-11

---

## Executive Summary

**3rbst** (عربست) is a micro-SaaS service that helps Arabic speakers in Germany understand their German documents through WhatsApp. Users send a photo of any German document, and AI analyzes it and explains it in Arabic.

**Key Metrics**:
- **Market Size**: ~2 million Arabic speakers in Germany
- **Pain Point**: Language barrier, expensive translators
- **Solution**: Instant AI-powered document analysis via WhatsApp
- **Revenue Model**: Pay-per-use + subscription
- **Gross Margin**: ~85%

---

## Problem Statement

### The Pain Point

Arabic speakers in Germany face significant challenges with German bureaucracy:

| Scenario | Pain Point | Traditional Solution | 3rbst Solution |
|----------|------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Mail from Finanzamt | Can't understand tax notice | Hire translator (€50-100) | Send photo → €1-3 |
| Court summons | Legal urgency, unclear what to do | Lawyer (€200+/hour) | Send photo → €1-3 |
| Medical bill | Unclear charges | Ask friend/wait | Send photo → instant |
| Rental contract | Don't understand terms | Legal review (€100+) | Send photo → €1-3 |
| Parking ticket | Unsure if legitimate | Pay anyway/fight | Send photo → know rights |

### Market Opportunity

**Demographics**:
- Syrian refugees: ~800,000
- Other Arabic speakers: ~1.2M (Iraqi, Palestinian, Lebanese, Egyptian)
- Total addressable market: ~2 million people
- Target segment: 20% (400K) digitally savvy, WhatsApp users

| Legal consultation | €200+/hour | 99% cheaper |

## Competitive Advantage

| Factor | Google Translate | 3rbst |

### vs. Competitors

**No direct competitors** in WhatsApp-based German→Arabic document analysis.

**Adjacent competitors**:
- Legal insurance apps (too expensive)
- Translation apps (too generic)
