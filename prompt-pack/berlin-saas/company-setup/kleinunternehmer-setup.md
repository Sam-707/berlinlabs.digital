# Kleinunternehmer Setup Guide
## Berlin Solopreneur Digital Solutions

---

## What is Kleinunternehmer?

Under **§ 19 UStG** (German VAT Act), you're a Kleinunternehmer if:

```
Your revenue in the first year is ≤ €22,000
AND
Your revenue in the second year is expected to be ≤ €50,000
```

**Benefits:**
- ❌ No VAT (Umsatzsteuer) to charge
- ❌ No VAT returns to file
- ✅ Simpler accounting
- ✅ Less paperwork

**Limitations:**
- ❌ Cannot deduct input VAT (Vorsteuer)
- ❌ Cannot voluntarily opt into VAT
- ⚠️ Customers outside Germany may miss VAT invoices

## Step 1: Gewerbeanmeldung (Business Registration)

### Before You Start (Checklist)

- [ ] Choose your company name
- [ ] Determine business activities
- [ ] Have your passport/ID ready
- [ ] Have €20-40 for registration fee
- [ ] Know your start date

### Book Appointment

**In Berlin:**
- **Einwohnermeldeamt** (Registration Office) where you're registered
- Often called "Gewerbeamt" (Trade Office)
- **Appointment:** Usually required (book online)
- **Fee:** €20-40 (varies by district)

**Berlin Districts (Bezirksämter):**
- Mitte: https://www.berlin.de/ba-mitte/
- Friedrichshain-Kreuzberg: https://www.berlin.de/ba-friedrichshain-kreuzberg/
- Pankow: https://www.berlin.de/ba-pankow/
- Charlottenburg-Wilmersdorf: https://www.berlin.de/ba-charlottenburg-wilmersdorf/
- [Find your district](https://www.berlin.de/ba-friedrichshain-kreuzberg/en/)

### What to Bring

```
Required Documents:
✅ Valid passport or ID card
✅ Completed registration form (provided at office)
✅ €20-40 in cash (often cash-only)
✅ If applicable: Trade permit (Gewerbeerlaubnis) for regulated industries

Not Required (but good to have):
→ Business plan (1 page is enough)
→ Printout of your planned website/landing page
```

### The Registration Form

**Company Name:**
```
Berlin Solopreneur Digital Solutions
[Your Name] Inhaber

Example: "Sam Smith Digital Solutions, Sam Smith Inhaber"
```

**Business Activities (Gegenstand des Gewerbes):**
```
Dienstleistungen im Bereich Softwareentwicklung, Webentwicklung
und digitale Produkte. Bereitstellung von Entwicklungsvorlagen
und Ressourcen für Softwareentwickler.

(Translation: Services in software development, web development
and digital products. Providing development templates and
resources for software developers.)
```

**Legal Form:**
```
☑️ Einzelgewerbe (Sole proprietorship)
☑️ Keine eingetragene Form (No registered form like GmbH)
```

## Step 2: Finanzamt Registration (Tax Office)

### After Gewerbeanmeldung

Within a few weeks of registering your business, you'll receive a **questionnaire from Finanzamt** called:
- **Fragebogen zur steuerlichen Erfassung** (Tax registration questionnaire)

### Key Questions on the Form

**1. Gewerbliche Tätigkeit / selbständige Arbeit:**
```
☑️ Gewerblich (Commercial activity)
```

**2. Gegenstand der Tätigkeit (Business activity):**
```
Softwareentwicklung, Webentwicklung, Verkauf von digitalen
Ressourcen und Entwicklungsvorlagen für SaaS-Produkte.
```

**3. Beginn der Tätigkeit (Start date):**
```
Your Gewerbeanmeldung date (date you registered)
```

**4. Kleinunternehmerregelung (§ 19 UStG):**

```
☑️ JA - Ich beabsichtige, die Kleinunternehmerregelung in Anspruch zu nehmen
☐ NEIN

Hinweis: If you check JA, you confirm:
- Expected revenue in 1st year ≤ €22,000
- Expected revenue in 2nd year ≤ €50,000
```

**5. Steuernummer (Tax Number):**

After processing (2-4 weeks), you'll receive:
- **Steuernummer** (Tax number) - Keep this safe!
- **Formular für Steuervereinfachung** (Form for simplified tax reporting)

## Step 3: Banking & Accounting

### Open a Business Bank Account

**Options:**

| Bank | Monthly Fee | Features |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **N26 Business** | Free | Mobile-first, English support |
| **DKB Business** | Free | Good for freelancers |
| **Commerzbank** | €5-10/month | Traditional, physical locations |
| **Postbank** | €5-9/month | Basic business account |
| **bunq** | Free/€9.99 | Easy opening, mobile |

**Documents Needed:**
- Gewerbeanmeldung copy
- ID/passport
- Proof of address (Meldebescheinigung)
- Tax number (if received yet)

**N26 Business** is often easiest for Berlin-based founders:
- Fully online
- English app
- Free business account
- Visa card included

### Accounting Setup

**Minimal Setup for Kleinunternehmer:**

```
Income (Einnahmen):
- Date
- Source (customer/platform)
- Amount (gross)
- Description

Expenses (Ausgaben):
- Date
- Category (software, hosting, marketing, etc.)
- Amount
- Receipt (keep digital or paper)
```

**Tools (Free/Low Cost):**
- **Lexoffice** - From €5/month (German, tailored for Kleinunternehmer)
- **sevDesk** - From €7.90/month
- **Sorted** - Free tier available
- **Excel/Google Sheets** - Free manual option

## Step 5: Website & Legal Pages

### Required Legal Pages (Germany)

Even as Kleinunternehmer, you need:

**1. Impressum (Legal Notice)**
```
Angaben gemäß § 5 TMG

Berlin Solopreneur Digital Solutions
Inhaber: [Your Full Name]
[Your Street Address]
[Your PLZ] Berlin
Germany

Kontakt:
E-Mail: [Your Email]
Website: [Your Website]

Umsatzsteuer-ID:
Gemäß § 19 UStG wird keine Umsatzsteuer berechnet.

(Sales Tax ID: Pursuant to § 19 UStG, no sales tax is charged.)

Verantwortlich für den Inhalt nach § 55 Abs. 2 RStV:
[Your Full Name]
[Your Address]
```

**2. Datenschutz (Privacy Policy)**
```
Since you're collecting emails/processing data, you need a privacy policy.
Use generators like:
→ https://www.datenschutz-generator.de/
→ https://itrlegal.de/datenschutz-generator-de/

Key points:
- What data you collect
- How you use it
- Contact for privacy questions
- SSL/HTTPS
- No data sold to third parties
```

**3. Terms of Service (Optional but recommended)**
```
For free products, keep it simple:
- License (MIT for code)
- No warranty
- Not liable for damages
```

## Step 7: First Year Tax Obligations

### As Kleinunternehmer, You Must:

1. **Income Tax Return (Einkommensteuererklärung)**
   - Due annually (usually by July 31 of following year)
   - Report all revenue minus expenses
   - File with ELSTER (online tax system)

2. **Trade Tax Return (Gewerbesteuererklärung)**
   - Only if profit exceeds €24,500
   - Most Kleinunternehmer don't pay this

3. **Advance Income Tax Payments (Einkommensteuer-Vorauszahlung)**
   - Finanzamt may request quarterly advance payments
   - Based on expected profit
   - Adjusted annually

### You DON'T Need:

- ❌ VAT returns (Umsatzsteuervoranmeldung)
- ❌ VAT ID (Umsatzsteuer-ID) - You can get one but don't need it
- ❌ Charge VAT on invoices

## Step 9: Revenue Tracking (Stay Under Limits)

### Track Your Progress

```
Kleinunternehmer Limits:
Year 1: ≤ €22,000
Year 2: ≤ €50,000

Monthly tracking:
Month | Revenue | Year Total
------|---------|-----------
Jan   | €0      | €0
Feb   | €0      | €0
Mar   | €500    | €500
...

If you approach limits:
→ Notify Finanzamt immediately
→ You'll become VAT-registered next year
→ Start charging 19% VAT
```

### What Counts as Revenue:

✅ **YES (Revenue):**
- Product sales
- Service fees
- Consulting income
- Course sales
- Subscription revenue

❌ **NO (Not Revenue):**
- Loans/investments received
- Private sales (selling your old laptop)
- Gifts

## Step 10: Quarterly Checklist

### Every 3 Months

- [ ] **Reconcile accounts** - Match bank statements to income/expenses
- [ ] **Save invoices** - Keep digital copies of all invoices (sent + received)
- [ ] **Track revenue** - Ensure you're under Kleinunternehmer limits
- [ ] **Pay tax advances** - If requested by Finanzamt
- [ ] **Backup data** - Save accounting data to cloud storage

### Annually

- [ ] **File income tax return** (Einkommensteuererklärung)
- [ ] **File trade tax return** (Gewerbesteuererklärung) if required
- [ ] **Check insurance coverage**
- [ ] **Review business registration** (update if address changes)
