## Step 8: Engagement Guidelines

### Reply to Every Comment (First Hour)

**When someone comments:**

Positive/Simple:
- "Thanks [Name]! 👋"
- "Appreciate that!"
- "Building in public, right?"

Question:
- Give thoughtful answer
- Maybe create follow-up post

Feedback:
- Thank them
- Ask follow-up questions
- Consider for improvements

Negative:
- Stay professional
- Acknowledge if valid
- Don't argue publicly

### Comment on Other Posts

**Daily goal: 5-10 meaningful comments on:**
- Berlin tech posts
- Indie hacking posts
- Web development posts
- SaaS founder posts

**Comment templates:**
```
"Great insights! We've been using [approach] with our Berlin SaaS community and found [result]. Have you tried [related thing]?"

"As a Berlin-based solopreneur, this resonates. We just launched a free prompt pack for shipping SaaS faster if anyone's interested."
```
